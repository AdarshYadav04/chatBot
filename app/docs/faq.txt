Q1: How do I shorten a URL?

A: Sign in → open Dashboard → click "Create Short URL" → enter the original URL → optionally add a custom alias → click Save.
Your short link will look like:
https://sho.rt/abc123

Q2: How can I view clicks or analytics for my short links?

A: Go to your Dashboard and click "View Stats" next to the link. You will see:

total clicks

country-wise clicks

device types

referrer websites
Location is inferred from IP and may not be fully accurate.

Q3: Can I create a custom alias?

A: Yes. Aliases must be unique and can contain:
A–Z, a–z, 0–9, hyphen (-), underscore (_)
If an alias is already taken, choose a different one.

Q4: Can I set an expiration date for my short URL?

A: Yes. During creation, you can choose an expiry date.
After expiration, the link will return 404 Not Found.

Q5: How does the service handle privacy and GDPR?

A: We store:

click timestamps

IP-derived geolocation (country/city)
We do not store full IP addresses beyond 30 days.
For deletion requests, contact: support@example.com

Q6: Why am I getting a 404 after creating a short URL?

A: Check the following:

Your domain is correct

DNS is properly set up

If using a custom domain, ensure you configured a CNAME record pointing to our service.
